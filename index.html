About Us

MyOnlineMeals is an Indian Online restaurant aggregator and food delivery company founded by Prakhar Dev in 2022. MyOnlineMeals provides information, menus and user-reviews of restaurants as well as food delivery options from partner restaurants in select cities. The service is available in 5 countries and in more than 10,000 cities.
